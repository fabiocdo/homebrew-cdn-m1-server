# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

This policy applies to use, distribution, and operation of this project.

## Scope

- This AUP is a project policy for official channels/resources (repository, issue tracker, docs, and maintainers' support surfaces).
- It does not replace, override, or add restrictions to rights granted by `LICENSE` (GNU GPLv3).
- If there is any conflict, `LICENSE` governs software copyright permissions.

## Allowed

- Personal, educational, research, and community use.
- Modifying, self-hosting, and redistributing the software under `LICENSE`.
- Hosting content you are legally authorized to distribute.

## Not Allowed

- Uploading/serving content without proper rights or authorization.
- Sharing copyrighted/proprietary game files without permission.
- Using the software to facilitate piracy or illegal content exchange.
- Bypassing legal restrictions, DRM, or platform protections where prohibited by law.
- Any malicious, abusive, fraudulent, or harmful activity.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may:

- Remove links, documentation references, or public support for deployments associated with abuse.
- Close issues/PRs that request or promote prohibited use.
- Preserve evidence from project channels when abuse reports are filed.
- Enforce policy only in project-controlled channels/resources.

## Compliance Reminder

Operators are responsible for implementing their own moderation, access controls,
logging, and legal compliance process for their instance.
Maintainers do not monitor or police third-party/self-hosted instances.
